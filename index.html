Bio

I am a student developer and researcher at Korea International School, deeply interested in the intersection of artificial intelligence, economics, and ethics. My work focuses on developing practical mobile applications and utilizing machine learning for financial forecasting and social utility. I am the Creator and Lead Developer of SnapPrice, an Android app utilizing OCR and ML to empower rational consumer consumption, and I serve as the Director of the Korea Chapter for Curiosity Creators, providing STEM education for young students globally.

My recent research applies advanced AI architectures to complex economic problems, such as combining Transformers and LLMs to solve the "Persistence Trap" in stock prediction and utilizing hybrid LSTM-LLM frameworks to forecast macroeconomic indicators like the 10-Year Treasury Yield.
